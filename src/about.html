About Us

At Majadtek, we’re not only
building technology, we’re
building the future

Majadtek Nigeria, incorporated in 2008, is a dynamic technology company committed to making Information and Communication Technology accessible to everyone. We focus on delivering high-quality solutions that simplify everyday challenges and support growth across multiple sectors.

Driven by the fast-evolving world of ICT, we create practical, user-friendly software and services designed to enhance human capability and improve how people work, learn, and connect. Today, Majadtek stands at the forefront of innovative software development in Nigeria—building solutions tailored to real needs, without stress or complexity.

Our Mission

To develop uncompromised quality ICT solutions for demystifying life endeavour at all levels of human organization through unparalleled creativity, genuine innovativeness and continuous enhancement of existing systems.

Our Vision

To become a brand uniquely known as an icon of quality and the pace setter in indigenous ICT solutions development in Nigeria by the year 2020.
